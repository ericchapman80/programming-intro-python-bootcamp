# Project Roadmap

This bootcamp builds Track Career Analyzer in small increments.

## Time Expectation

- 10 phases
- 3-5 hours per week
- 25-40 total hours
- Flexible pacing

## Suggested Weekly Rhythm

- 30 minutes reading and concept review
- 45 minutes guided coding
- 45 minutes practice
- 1-2 hours weekend project and demo work

## Project Growth

The app begins as a small terminal program and grows into:

1. A printed welcome message.
2. An athlete profile.
3. Goal comparison logic.
4. Multiple saved results.
5. Cleaner function-based code.
6. CSV-backed persistence.
7. pandas-powered analysis.
8. Streamlit dashboard.
9. Shareable app.
10. Final demo project.
